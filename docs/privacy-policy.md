# Privacy Policy — Archive Lookup

_Last updated: March 15, 2026_

Archive Lookup is a Chrome extension that helps you find archived snapshots of web pages. This policy explains exactly what data the extension accesses and what it sends over the network.

---

## What the extension does

When you right-click a link and choose "Look up on Archive.today", the extension opens a new tab pointing to the most recent archived snapshot of that link.

---

## Permissions

The extension requests one Chrome permission:

| Permission     | Why it is needed                                                      |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `contextMenus` | To add the "Look up on Archive.today" right-click menu item on links. |

No host permissions, storage, or scripting permissions are requested.

---

## Information collection

**The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data about you.**

No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind is included.

---

## Data stored locally

The extension does not use any storage mechanism. No data is persisted.

---

## Network requests

The extension does not make any network requests itself. When you choose the context menu item, your browser opens a new tab navigating to the archive service's URL. This is a normal browser navigation — the archive service sees the request just as it would any other visitor.

There is no extension backend, no sync server, and no communication with any server operated by this extension.

---

## Third-party services

When you use the extension, your browser navigates to a web archive service that operates under its own terms and privacy policy. The extension has no control over the archive service's data practices.

---

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not read the content of pages you visit.
- It does not record your browsing history.
- It does not use cookies.
- It does not include any advertising or analytics code.
- It does not share any data with the extension developer or any third party.

---

## Children's privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

---

## Changes to this policy

If the extension is updated in a way that changes how it handles data, this policy will be updated. The date at the top of this document reflects when it was last revised.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the extension's behavior, please open an issue in the project's repository.
